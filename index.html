Grilled Cheese Sandwich Ingredients 4 slices white bread or sourdough 4 ounces cheddar cheese or American cheese 3 Tablespoons of Butter 2 tablespoons shredded cheddar optional Instructions Preheat a small skillet over low heat. Add Butter and melt Place 2 slices of bread in the skillet. Top with cheddar cheese slices, black pepper to taste and remaining slice of bread, mayonnaise side out. Grill until golden, about 4-5 minutes. Flip and grill the other side until golden. Optional: Just before serving, add the shredded cheddar directly to the outside of the sandwich and grill an additional minute or so until it gets crispy.
